SAFT-LLE Demo

SAFT

SAFT, or Statistical Associating Fluid Theory, is a semiemperical equation of state that is founded on the basis of Wertheim's first-order perturbation theory. SAFT describes the Helmholtz free energy as the sum of a segment-segment interaction term, a chain-chain term, and an association term.1

In this current model, a series of approximations are implemented to provide a quick-running demo of a SAFT LLE calculation. The approximations implemented are as follows:

  1. The molecules are not chains ie) no chain term
  2. Approximation as hard spheres
  3. Each association site is indistinguishable
